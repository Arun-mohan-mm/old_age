About Us

Welcoming you to see & shop all the Senior citizens necessities peacefully

Dear Seniors,
Namaskaram..
We the Goldenage4u.com is a philanthropic initiative of a team of healthcare professionals from Kochi, Kerala. We received the seed of this thought once we really see the necessities and difficulties by senior citizens of the society to manage and to arrange medical and associated needs during Covid time. After that, we kerala is affected by sudden climatic changes which results in extreme hot and extreme rain. So we are trying to help you by combining online store facility through our website Goldenage4u.com and physical delivery to your address through us. We are supporting all over India particularly the state of Kerala. You are very much welcome to find the necessary products through our website or any other online or offline sources and call us for buying the same. Let's join together for an easy and hassle free retirement life. Best wishes from the team Goldenage4u.com.
